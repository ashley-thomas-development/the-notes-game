Play The Arnold Game!

Instructions:

Welcome! This is a simple memory game with fun distractions.

At the beginning of each turn a sound will play. It is your job to repeat the pattern back. On each new turn you will only see and hear Arnold's new choice, while you still have to input the entire pattern from the beginning. And don't take Arnold too seriously. Good Luck!

Limit Meter:

Depending on how quickly you are able to complete Arnold's pattern, your meter may fill.

Once it reaches the top you have a choice of two special powers to use whenever you wish. If your meter is filled when you lose, you carry the powers over to the next game.

The "Clear" button will help if you start entering in Arnold's pattern and lose your place. The "Clear" button resets only what you have entered this round.

The "Replay" button will play back Arnold's whole pattern, so listen close!

Options:

If you want an extra challenge, "Party Mode" will continually change the colors of the squares, and get faster with every turn.

Some items on the screen can be double-clicked to make their color slowly change. Single-click to stop the color where it's at.

Tips:

We humans are good at remembering small chunks of info. Think about how we group information like phone numbers. Use this to your advantage! Try remembering the pattern in smaller groups of 3, or even 5!
