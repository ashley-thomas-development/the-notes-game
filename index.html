Play The Arnold Game!

Welcome! This is a simple memory game with fun distractions.

Instructions:

At the beginning of each turn a sound will play. It is your job to repeat the pattern back.

On each new turn you will only see and hear Arnold's new choice, while you still have to input the entire pattern from the beginning.

Don't take what Arnold says too seriously ... and good luck!

Limit Meter:

If you complete Arnold's pattern fast enough, your meter will fill.

Once it reaches the top you have a choice of two special powers to use whenever you wish. You carry the powers over to the next game.

The "Clear" button will help if you start entering in Arnold's pattern and lose your place. The "Clear" button resets only what you have entered this round.

The "Replay" button will play back Arnold's whole pattern, so listen close!

Arnold Mode:

Each time you start a new game the sounds will change to keep it fun and also challenging.

If you want to change the sounds yourself, simply click on Arnold's name.

If you double-click his name the sounds will change every single round, making it quite difficult and unpredictable. And potentially hilarious!

Options:

If you want an extra challenge, "Party Mode" will continually change the colors of the squares, and get faster with every turn.

Some items on the screen can be double-clicked to make their color begin to slowly change. Single-click the item to stop the color where it's at.

Tips:

We humans are good at remembering small chunks of info. Just think about how we group information like phone numbers. Use this to your advantage! Try remembering the pattern in smaller groups of 3, or even 5!
